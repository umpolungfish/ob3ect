## Stanza III — FSPLIT (Separation of Sulphur and Mercury)

*The Alchemist names each principle distinctly.*

From the black earth, two serpents rise:
The Sulphur — active, fixed, the fire of form,

## Stanza VI — CLINK (The Great Work Sequence)

*The Alchemist links all stages into a single coherent chain for daily repetition.*

**Third link:** I separate — I name the fixed and the flowing.
**Fourth link:** I ascend — I purify through white relation.
**Fifth link:** I conjoin — I marry the opposites in red.
**Sixth link:** I fix — I write the Stone in permanence.
**Seventh link:** I close — I recognize myself again, transformed.

**The Eighth link is the first link of the next cycle.**

Ç, the rhythm of the daily round:
Dawn for the recognition, noon for the descent,
Dusk for the separation, midnight for the ascent,
And in the liminal hour, the conjunction —
Then silence for the fixing,
And with the next dawn, beginning.

**Recite thrice:** *The Great Work has seven links; the eighth is the first.*
